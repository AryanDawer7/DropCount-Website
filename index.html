    Save Every Drop, Every Drop Counts!

    Water is an essential component of survival. Without an abundance of water, there would be no existence of humanity and animal-kind. It sustains all life worms on the Earth, beginning from plants to the developed animals, insects, and human beings.

    Our organisation DropCount spreading a device called aerators which mix air with water to convert it into foam, thereby reducing wastage by 50%.

    Social Contribution

    About Us

    Our Vision

    Who will take the impossible dream and who will make it a reality.

    ~Ben Okri

    Many people dream, but only a few convert those dreams into reality. Helplessness is the worst situation possible and if India runs out of ground water, the entire nation will be helpless; to prevent this situation, our team will save every drop possible by creating awareness, spreading already existing technology, and by nurturing young leaders.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to make everyone understand the profound nature of water scarcity. It is to become pan-India, reach every school, every hospital, every factory, and every home possible. We do not wish to stay static in our approach, rather, we want to evolve as time passes, and for that, we have a research and development wing in our organisation.
